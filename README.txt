Are you indoors or outdoors (as a person)?

This is a simple quiz game which is to ask you a series of questions to determine if you are an indoor or an outdoor
individual! You will be asked whether or not you are ready to start the game upon clicking in order to give you immediate access
to the game! Answering with a lower case 'y' for 'yes' will provide you with a welcome message (like "Awesome!"). A lowercase 'n' for 'no'
will give you a see you later message.

UPDATE: Base game has been completed 5/16/2015 . No styling or anything as of yet.
